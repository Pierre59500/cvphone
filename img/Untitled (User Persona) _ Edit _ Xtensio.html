Frequently Asked Questions

What is Xtensio?
Xtensio is a creative collaboration platform to design living documents, work together with internal and external teams, and flexibly share your work as digital presentations, responsive web pages or exportable PDF or PNGs.
What’s a folio?
Folios are full-screen digital presentations, responsive web pages and exportable PDF or PNGs all-in-one. A folio can be anything from a brief business strategy exercise to a full-length presentation. Start with one of our templates, or create anything you need with the blank slate tool.

Your folios are live pages that you can edit and collaborate on with colleagues, clients and other key players. All of your changes—from dragging and dropping modules, to editing graphs, to changing colors— will save and sync instantly across devices. Here’s more on Making a Folio.
Do I have limits on the number of templates I can try?
No. You can try all of our free templates with any of our plans. The limitations are only on your active folios.
Is my data private and secure?
Yes. All Xtensio plans include 128 bit SSL encryption and backups to ensure your data is secure. Your work belongs to you. Your folios are private unless you decide to share them or make them public. Here’s more on the range of sharing options on Xtensio, from completely private to public.
Is Xtensio really free?
Yes. You will never be asked to provide credit card information for Xtensio’s free account.
What would I get by upgrading to a premium plan?
By upgrading to our premium plans, you’ll create a private and branded workspace with your own URL where you, your team members and guests can create and collaborate on multiple presentations and documents, lock branding across your deliverables with the team style guide, streamline the creation process with custom templates, unlock exports, add password protection and customize individual shared links, and view folio stats to stay updated on all of your projects. Here’s more about our premium plans.
What would I use the one pager template for?
Startups can use the one-pager template to present your business idea to different people. Unlike your website, which speaks to a general audience, one-page summary allow you to succinctly convey your message to a targeted audience. This will allow you to capture interest, identify potholes in your business plan and focus your team’s decision-making.
Do you have any examples I can use?
Yes! We have a couple of startup one pager examples above that you can use as a starting point for your own. If you find a style you like, use it for your inspiration. Just click on one of the examples further up on this page to save it as a folio and start editing with your own content.
How do I define my positioning statement?
A positioning statement is a one or two-sentence declaration that communicates your brand’s unique value to your customers in relation to your main competitors. You can find an exercise for establishing a strong positioning statement in our how-to guide.
Can I add colleagues to edit my one pager?
Yes! On the Business plan, you can invite as many people as you’d like to collaborate on your one pager, ensuring your whole team is aligned. Here’s more on real-time collaboration.
How do I share my one pager?
When you’re ready to share your one-page summary, choose between presenting it as a full-screen digital slideshow, sending it as a responsive web link or exporting your one pager as a PDF or PNG (premium feature). All of your share options are located at the top right of your edit screen. Learn more about the different ways to share your folios in this tutorial video.
Do you have other tools for showcasing my business?
We do! There are a number of different templates, examples and resources to guide you through the process of promoting your brand. Check out our Marketing toolbox and our Business Development toolbox to find more tools and resources.

Frequently asked questions

What is Xtensio?

Xtensio is the quickest and easiest way for teams to create, share and manage all of their business documents together. Create pitches, sales sheets, reports, case studies, agendas and more. Add images, videos, tables, buttons and other interactive modules. Drag and drop, resize, change backgrounds, colors. Share a link. Present a digital slideshow. Export a PDF/PNG.

What kind of documents can I create with Xtensio?

You can create any sort of business content you need - one-pagers, fact sheets, pitches and proposals, press releases, employee handbooks, reports, strategy canvases, landing pages and more. Check out our library of instructional templates and editable examples.

What’s the difference between a folio and a template?

Instructional templates guide you in creating a variety of business content. A folio is your personal copy made from a template. It’s a live page you can edit, collaborate on and share.

How do I share my work?

You can invite users to collaborate. You can present your work as a full-screen digital slideshow. Export it as a PNG or PDF (premium features). Share it as a live web link or embed it on your website. Xtensio is the most flexible platform for sharing business collateral.

Can I collaborate with people on individual plans?

You can try out unlimited collaboration on the free plan. However, individual premium plans (Basic and Plus) do not include collaboration. Check out the business plans for premium team access.

How is Xtensio different from PPT, Illustrator, Canva or Word?

Unlike other presentation or document creators, Xtensio works like a web builder so you can easily drag and drop interactive content, customize the layout, and change colors, fonts, backgrounds to get professional-looking business content out the door quickly, without needing a designer.


You can collaborate with team members and guests to work together in real-time on the cloud. And the flexibility to share folios as live web links, digital slideshow presentations or exportable PDF/PNGs means you don't have to jump between tools when creating different deliverables. Check out this page to see how Xtensio outpaces the competition.

What is a folio?

A folio is a live webpage, a full-screen digital slideshow presentation and an exportable PDF/PNG document all-in-one. Folios are responsive, collaborative and completely customizable.


Start from an instructional template, editable example or a blank folio to build all your business documents and presentations with the ease of a web builder. Add team members and guests to work together in real-time on the cloud. Changes instantly sync to the live version of the folio so both internal and external stakeholders are always up-to-date. Folios are the next generation of flexible documents and presentations.

Is the free plan really free?

Yes. You will never be asked to provide credit card information for Xtensio’s free account.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes. All Xtensio plans include 128 bit SSL encryption and backups to ensure your data is secure. You can make shared folio links private on premium plans. Here’s more on the range of sharing options on Xtensio, from completely private to public.

How do I upgrade?

It's simple. Pick one of our premium plans, enter your card info and start using Xtensio's premium features!


All payment data is encrypted and secure. We accept all major credits cards and plans are priced in USD.

Can I change or cancel my plan at any time?

Yes. You can upgrade, downgrade, switch between monthly or yearly options, or go back to a free plan at any time. To make changes, go to your Billing settings.


If you cancel your plan, premium features will remain active until the end of your current billing period and your account will then be downgraded back to the free plan.

What happens to my content when I cancel?

Once your account is downgraded to the free plan, you'll keep the last 3 folios your team edited. Any other active folios will automatically be moved into the archives for 2 weeks before they are permanently deleted. You can delete an active folio and recover an archived folio at any time before then, or upgrade and recover all of your folios.

Frequently asked questions

What is Xtensio?

Xtensio is the quickest and easiest way for teams to create, share and manage all of their business documents together. Create pitches, sales sheets, reports, case studies, agendas and more. Add images, videos, tables, buttons and other interactive modules. Drag and drop, resize, change backgrounds, colors. Share a link. Present a digital slideshow. Export a PDF/PNG.

What’s the difference between a folio and a template?

Instructional templates guide you in creating a variety of business content. A folio is your personal copy made from a template. It’s a live page you can edit, collaborate on and share.

How do I share my work?

You can invite users to collaborate. You can present your work as a full-screen digital slideshow. Export it as a PNG or PDF (premium features). Share it as a live web link or embed it on your website. Xtensio is the most flexible platform for sharing business collateral.

Can I collaborate with people on business plans?

Yes! All Business plans include team management and real-time collaboration. Add team members and guests to create and collaborate on your private workspace. Change user access, and set collaboration levels for channels (folders) and folios (documents) inside the team workspace.

How is Xtensio different from PPT, Illustrator, Canva or Word?

Unlike other presentation or document creators, Xtensio works like a web builder so you can easily drag and drop interactive content, customize the layout, and change colors, fonts, backgrounds to get professional-looking business content out the door quickly, without needing a designer.


You can collaborate with team members and guests to work together in real-time on the cloud. And the flexibility to share folios as live web links, digital slideshow presentations or exportable PDF/PNGs means you don't have to jump between tools when creating different deliverables. Check out this page to see how Xtensio outpaces the competition.

Do you have discounts for startups, nonprofits and Students and Educators users?

Yes! We have dedicated plans for education users with discounts already included. For nonprofits and startups, we offer 50% off Xtensio's Team yearly plan. Please fill out the nonprofit discount application or the startup discount application and we will review within 3 business days.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes. All Xtensio plans include 128 bit SSL encryption and backups to ensure your data is secure. You can make shared folio links private on premium plans. Here’s more on the range of sharing options on Xtensio, from completely private to public.

What is a folio?

A folio is a live webpage, a full-screen digital slideshow presentation and an exportable PDF/PNG document all-in-one. Folios are responsive, collaborative and completely customizable.


Start from an instructional template, editable example or a blank folio to build all your business documents and presentations with the ease of a web builder. Add team members and guests to work together in real-time on the cloud. Changes instantly sync to the live version of the folio so both internal and external stakeholders are always up-to-date. Folios are the next generation of flexible documents and presentations.

Do you offer free trials for Business plans?

We do not offer free trials at this time. You’re more than welcome to create a free account and try the platform for as long as you'd like before upgrading. Or you can always schedule a demo with our team.

How do I upgrade?

It's simple. Pick one of our premium plans, enter your card info and start using Xtensio's premium features!


All payment data is encrypted and secure. We accept all major credit cards and plans are priced in USD.

Can I pay by invoice?

For Agency and custom Enterprise plans, yes, we'll set up a payment by invoice. For other premium plans, we accept all major credit cards.

Can I change or cancel my plan at any time?

Yes. You can upgrade, downgrade, switch between monthly or yearly options, or go back to a free plan at any time. To make changes, go to your Billing settings.


If you cancel your plan, premium features will remain active until the end of your current billing period and your account will then be downgraded back to the free plan.

What happens to my content when I cancel?

Once your account is downgraded to the free plan, you'll keep the last 3 folios your team edited. Any other active folios will automatically be moved into the archives for 2 weeks before they are permanently deleted. You can delete an active folio and recover an archived folio at any time before then, or upgrade and recover all of your folios.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is Xtensio?
    Xtensio is a business communications platform where teams create, manage, and present beautiful living documents, called folios. Flexibly share your folios as digital presentations, responsive web pages or exportable PDF or PNGs.
    What’s the difference between Pro and Business?
    Key differences include the amount of folios (projects) per workspace and premium features such as customizing and/or password-protecting links. Pro includes 10 folios (projects) per workspace, while Business includes unlimited, making it our most popular.
    Can I switch betwen plans during the trial?
    During your trial, you can switch to our Free Forever plan or any paid plan at any time. However, you can not switch from one trial to another trial.
    Can I download a folio during the trial period?
    No, exports are a paid feature only. You will have access to digital sharing during the trial period (link sharing and presentations). To export PDF or PNG files, you can upgrade to a subscription plan to unlock unlimited exports or purchase export credits as a one-time purchase.
    Is there a free plan?
    Yes. All new accounts start with a premium trial. You can upgrade to a subscription plan, switch between plans or downgrade to the Free Forever plan at any time. You will never be asked for a credit card to use the Free Forever plan.
    What happens when my trial ends?
    Once your trial period ends, you can choose to enter payment information to continue using premium features or downgrade to our Free forever plan.
    Where can I view a full comparison of the plans?
    You see a comprehensive list of features and comparison chart on our pricing page
    Is my data private and secure?
    Yes. All Xtensio plans include 128 bit SSL encryption and backups to ensure your data is secure. Your work belongs to you. Your folios are private unless you decide to share them or make them public.
